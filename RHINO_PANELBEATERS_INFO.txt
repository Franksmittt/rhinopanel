WHO WE ARE
----------
Rhino Panelbeaters & Towing is a family-owned and operated collision repair 
centre based in Mtubatuba, KwaZulu-Natal, South Africa. Founded with a single 
tow truck and a passion for mechanics, we have grown into Mtubatuba's premier 
collision repair facility, serving the entire Zululand region and Northern Route.

We are a team of qualified artisans dedicated to restoring safety and value to 
your vehicle. We don't just fix cars; we help people get back on the road with 
confidence and peace of mind.

OUR STORY
---------
Founded with humble beginnings, Rhino Panelbeaters & Towing recognized a need 
in the Zululand region for a repairer that didn't compromise on quality. Today, 
we serve the Northern Route (Hluhluwe, Mkhuze, Pongola, Ulundi, Nongoma, up to 
Kosi Bay), combining old-school craftsmanship with modern chassis straightening 
technology and waterborne paint systems.
